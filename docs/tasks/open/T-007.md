# T-007: Test the Pilot in a Real Usage Scenario

## Status

- open

## Goal

- Validate whether guests understand the flow without explanation.

## Done when

- the pilot is tried in a real or realistic usage setting
- confusion points and failure points are recorded
- follow-up work is added to the task or decision docs

## In scope

- lightweight manual pilot testing
- observation notes
- documenting follow-up work

## Out of scope

- analytics pipelines
- backend telemetry
- broad product expansion

## Recommendations

- Confirm a first-time guest can understand the `Add yourself` path without explanation.
- Confirm a returning guest can spot themselves quickly in the active guest list and reach their tally with one tap.
- Confirm guests understand drink prices as reference information and do not expect a live checkout subtotal.
- Confirm public visibility of room numbers and guest names is acceptable for the pilot setting.
- Record whether the active guest list becomes stale or crowded during realistic use.
